Instructions:

  1. Click on Play to start the game.
  2. A random word will show up on th screen with start of a timer of 10 seconds.
  3. You have to type the same word in the text field before the timer runs out.
  4. Every time you enter the right word, the time increases by 5 seconds.
  5. As you score up in the game, the added time decreases from 5 seconds upto 1 second.
  6. If the timer hits zero, GAME OVER!
